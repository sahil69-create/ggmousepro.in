Support & FAQ

Got questions? We've got answers.

Will I get banned for using this?

GG Mouse Pro is designed to be safe and uses non-intrusive overlay technology. However, always check the specific game's terms of service.

Does it support iOS?

Currently, GG Mouse Pro is exclusive to Android. iOS support is in development.

What Android versions are supported?

GG Mouse Pro supports Android 10 and above. Performance may vary on older devices.

Is the app free?

The core features are free. Some premium features may require a subscription or one-time purchase.

My mouse isn't connecting, what do I do?

Ensure OTG is enabled in your phone settings if using wired. For Bluetooth, unpair and pair again. Check our video guide in the app.

How do I create a custom mapping profile?

Open the app, select your game, and tap 'Add Profile'. Drag the keys to the corresponding on-screen buttons and save.
